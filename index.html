About Me

Hi! I'm Edi Ajiatas — a musician with roots in the Bay Area and a passion for creating connections through music!

My love for music began unexpectedly at age 15, during a visit to a church in Redwood City. I didn't understand the lyrics at first, but I was instantly drawn to the music — an experience that sparked a lasting passion.

As my family became more involved with the church, the band offered a chance to learn an instrument, I jumped in and chose the guitar. From there, I immersed myself in learning by ear, watching YouTube tutorials, and studying music theory on my own.

Today, I have over 10 years of experience on guitar and five years on bass, playing in both church and professional settings.

Music isn't just something I do — it's how I connect, grow, and share joy with others. Whether I'm on stage, in the studio, or experimenting with new ideas at home, I'm always chasing that spark that first inspired me.

Thanks for stopping by — feel free to reach out, collaborate, or just say hello.
